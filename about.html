About Us

Welcome to Coastal Machali

Coastal Machali Co. is a fine dining outlet that specializes in seafood. It offers a wide variety of dishes from the coastal regions of India. The menu features a selection of fresh seafood, cooked in traditional Indian spices and herbs. The restaurant also offers a range of vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes. The atmosphere is relaxed and inviting, with a warm and friendly staff.
